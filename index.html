Fun fact: I wrote my first piece of code in C and it was not "Hello World".

Hi, my name is Rafiya and I'm a web developer with a passion for front end development and design. My main expertise is in front-end development, which includes a firm understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I also have been expanding my skills in different modern frameworks, libraries, and tools.
I first started my journey as a developer when I built a simple invoicing app for a neighbourhood store. And once that happened, I took up small projects and worked remotely as a freelancer. Over the past few years I had many oppurtunities to work for indivuduals and companies around the globe where I met and learnt from amazing and ambitious people.
I currently work at Algotrady, an algorithmic tarding platform, where I built UIs using React. I aspire towards a career that will allow me to channel my creativity through crafting beautiful software and engaging experiences.

As an individual, I am a driven self learner, who is motivated to become an efficient, result oriented developer. When I'm not on the computer, I enjoy reading a new book, learning handlettering or writing my food blog.
